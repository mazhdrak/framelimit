Affiliate Disclosure

How FRAMELIMIT earns revenue and why it never influences our editorial decisions.

Last updated: March 2026

Amazon Associates Program

FRAMELIMIT (framelimit.com) is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

When you click an Amazon link on this site and make a purchase, we earn a small commission — typically 1–4% of the sale price — at no extra cost to you. The price you pay is exactly the same whether you use our link or navigate to Amazon directly.

Our Amazon Associate tag is framelimit20-20. You will see this tag in the URLs of all Amazon links on this site.

What This Means for Our Reviews

Our affiliate relationships never influence our editorial content. We do not:

  • Accept payment for positive reviews or higher scores
  • Prioritise recommending products based on commission rates
  • Receive free products in exchange for favourable coverage
  • Accept sponsored placements or paid rankings

Every laptop score, recommendation, and ranking on this site is based entirely on our own testing and analysis. If a product scores poorly, we say so — regardless of whether it earns us a commission.

Other Affiliate Programs

In addition to Amazon Associates, FRAMELIMIT may participate in affiliate programs with other retailers such as Best Buy, Newegg, or direct manufacturer programs. All affiliate links are identified with rel="nofollow sponsored" attributes.

Price Accuracy

Prices displayed on this site are estimates based on data available at the time of writing. Actual prices change frequently. Always click through to Amazon or the retailer to see the current price before making a purchase decision.

FTC Compliance

This disclosure is provided in accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's 16 CFR Part 255 guidelines on the use of endorsements and testimonials in advertising. We are required by law to disclose any material connections between ourselves and the products we review or recommend.

Questions

If you have questions about our affiliate relationships or how we make money, please contact us. We're happy to be transparent.
