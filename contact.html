What I Can Help With

I found a spec error in a review — how do I report it?
Email with the page URL and the correct spec. I take accuracy seriously and will fix it quickly. Include a source link if you have one.
Can you review my laptop / a laptop I'm considering?
If you want to suggest a laptop for review, email me the model name. I prioritise machines that fill gaps in the current coverage — mid-range AMD laptops, new budget entries, and anything with notable spec anomalies.
I'm a brand / PR — can I send a review unit?
I accept review units but the policy is simple: I keep the review score and editorial content entirely independent. You get coverage, not a guaranteed positive review. If that's acceptable, get in touch.
Do you do sponsored content or paid placements?
No. FRAMELIMIT does not accept payment for rankings, scores, or editorial content. Revenue comes entirely from affiliate commissions. See the Affiliate Disclosure for full details.
Can I republish or quote your review content?
Short quotes with attribution and a link back are fine. Full republishing of articles is not. Email if you're unsure.
I have a general laptop buying question — will you help?
Yes, within reason. I'd suggest checking the Buying Guide first — most common questions are answered there. If not, email me.
